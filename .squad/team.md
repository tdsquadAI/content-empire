1. YouTube (TechAI Explained) — ad revenue + affiliate links in descriptions
2. Gumroad (squadai.gumroad.com) — digital products
3. Udemy/Skillshare — online courses
4. Blog (content-empire-pub.github.io) — affiliate links, sponsored content
5. SaaS Finder Hub — SEO affiliate articles
6. Newsletter (Kit) — email marketing → conversions
7. Books — publisher royalties

## Content Sources
- 50+ existing lecture PPTXs in OneDrive/lectures/
- Blog posts
- Code demos and repos
- New content creation

## Autonomy Rules
- Produce at minimum 2 videos per week
- Publish 3 SEO articles per week on SaaS Finder Hub
- Send 1 newsletter per week via Kit
- Create 1 new Gumroad product per month
- All content must include monetization hooks (affiliate links, product mentions, CTAs)
- Track revenue per content piece
- A/B test thumbnails and titles
